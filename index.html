Forged by Knowledge

Driven by ambition and defined by excellence, Episteme is the first-ever academic fest of AIIMS Deoghar. It represents a collective commitment to scientific rigor, intellectual curiosity, and evidence-based medicine.

About Episteme

Episteme is envisioned as a flagship academic platform that bridges the gap between curriculum-based learning and exploratory scientific pursuit. It brings together students and faculty in a shared space dedicated to research discussion, academic discourse, and innovation.

Theme

Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

The 2026 edition of Episteme focuses on the transformative role of artificial intelligence in medicine — from diagnostics and clinical decision-making to research, innovation, and future healthcare delivery.

Symposium

Episteme 2026 features a multidisciplinary academic symposium comprising:

  • MEDISCOPE – Clinical Case Presentation
  • CONCEPTUM – Research Idea Presentation
  • SCHOLARIUM – Research Paper Presentation
  • PROTOMED – Medical Model Presentation
  • CLINOVATE – Hands-on Workshops
